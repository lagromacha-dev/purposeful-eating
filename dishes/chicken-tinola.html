Chicken Tinola

Category: Skin & Joints (Cheapest Collagen Support)

What Chicken Tinola Is

Chicken Tinola is a light Filipino soup made with skin-on chicken, ginger, and vegetables simmered in clear broth.

Why People Turn to Tinola

Chosen for comfort, warmth, and gentle nourishment.

Why It Supports Skin & Joints

  • Chicken skin and bones contain collagen
  • Gentle simmering releases collagen into the broth

When Tinola Is Most Helpful

  • Everyday meals
  • Recovery days
  • Sensitive digestion

How to Cook Chicken Tinola

Ingredients

  • Chicken (with skin and bones)
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Water
  • Green papaya or sayote
  • Fish sauce or salt

Steps

  1. Sauté ginger and onion.
  2. Add chicken and lightly brown.
  3. Add water and simmer until tender.
  4. Add vegetables and season lightly.

Purposeful Eating Note

Consistency matters more than intensity.
