Sinigang

Category: Gut Comfort (Best Option)

What Sinigang Is

Sinigang is a sour Filipino soup made with tamarind, vegetables, and fish or pork.

Why It Supports Gut Comfort

  • Sourness stimulates digestion
  • Warm broth is easy on the stomach

How to Cook Sinigang

Ingredients

  • Fish or pork
  • Tamarind
  • Vegetables
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil protein in water.
  2. Add tamarind and vegetables.
  3. Season lightly and serve.
